About Us

UCL Space Geodesy & Navigation Laboratory (SGNL) specializes in the development of radiation force models for spacecraft. Our work enables precise orbit determination and supports spacecraft navigation for a variety of missions.

  • SpaceCraft Visualizer

    JavaScript-based spacecraft model visualizer.

  • SRP-TRR Classic

    C++-based solar radiation pressure and thermal re-radiation calculations.

  • Grid Utilities

    Grid File Generator. This model resamples the outputs of SRP-TRR Classic to form grid files that can be read into orbit propagation/determination routines.

  • ODL22

    The 2022 version of our full-fidelity C++ orbit propagator.
